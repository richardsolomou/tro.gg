# World

Setting, tone, and story are deliberately undecided. tro.gg leads with functionality; canon gets figured out later — and much of it is written by the tribe as the world is built and played, not pre-written here.

Two things are fixed:

- **Troggs** are the player characters. Lowercase in prose; `player` in code and schema.
- **Hogs** — hedgehogs — are friendly NPCs: merchants, companions, townsfolk who run the hub town. A warm, cosmetic nod to PostHog — they're characters in the world, not mascots for features, and product dashboards stay out of the fiction.

Everything else — zone names, items, monsters, landmarks, story, and art direction — is open.
