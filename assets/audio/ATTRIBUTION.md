## Notes

- Do not ship the whole folder blindly. Pick short one-shots, normalize levels, and trim silence before wiring playback.
- Prefer OGG for browser playback where available; keep FLAC/WAV files as source-quality masters when they are already present.
- If replacing the Freesound preview MP3s with originals later, keep the same source rows above and update the filenames.
